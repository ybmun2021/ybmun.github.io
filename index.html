Welcome to YBMUN 2021!

Dear delegates and future diplomats,

It is my greatest privilege to welcome you all to Yuvabharathi Public School’s very first Intra-Model UN conference - YBMUN 2021, set for the 10th and 11th of September 2021. Model UN is a simulation of the international peace-keeping body United Nations, and as delegates we get to have a glimpse of being diplomats and ambassadors of countries all over the world.

My MUN experience began almost 5 years ago and I have been passionate about it ever since. Model UN is not only about coming together to discuss a global affair, it encompasses so much more. When you represent a country, you don’t just speak from your opinion, rather we adopt the views of that nation and its people’s emotions. It has helped me gain insight on several global affairs, but most importantly, it’s given me a whole new perspective. Model UN has been one of my greatest learning experiences, and it has made me who I am today. Attending conferences like these not only makes you a better diplomat and a better leader, but helps you realize your role as a global citizen. Moreover, it is one of the best platforms to meet new people.,

We are conducting this virtual conference with the hopes of introducing our students to the world of MUN-ing and providing a training platform for all first-timers. We believe that starting with an intra-MUN might just be the starting push to cultivate the interest in debating and encourage more students to participate in future conferences. Although a virtual one, we have made every effort to give our delegates a unique and stimulating conference. We assure all delegates that this will truly be an invigorating and rewarding experience, and that our organizing team and I will be with them through every step of the way.

Hosting our very own conference has been a long-awaited opportunity for us and it brings me immense pleasure to be a part of this journey.
Having said that, I hope all of you have a marvellous experience with us. I wish the best of luck to all our delegates!

Kritheega Ramanathan
Secretary-General
Yuvabharathi Model United Nations
secgen@ybmun.in

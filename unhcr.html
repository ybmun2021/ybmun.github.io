Chairperson

Shania K Pillai

Hi everyone, I'm Shania, a proud alumna of Yuvabharathi Public School. I love to read and spend most of my time with my nose in my phone, reading just about anything I can get my hands on. I pride myself on being a good orator. However, my social battery tends to be in power-saving mode all the time. I am not exceptionally gifted, nor am I exceptionally bright, but I am very inquisitive. Personally, that's what got me into MUNning, and I have never regretted it. I am an ardent MUNner, with over five years of experience and lots of memories to cherish from it. I hope that this MUN experience can be as memorable to you all as it always will be to me. I'm counting down the days until D-day, and I'm looking forward to witnessing quality debate.

UNDP

Agenda:Advancing Poverty Alleviation Through Inclusive Digitisation and Equitable Access.

Chairperson

Sharafia Ahmed

Sharafia Ahamed is a first-year Computer Engineering student at Kumaraguru College of Technology, with a great fervour towards MUNs over five years, chairing in cities like Tirupur, Coimbatore and Chennai. With a focus on committees such as UNHCR and UNCSW, she is an avid debater who has won awards in various MUNs. She demonstrates professionalism, expertise, and dedication in her role as chair. Apart from debates in a professional setting, quizzing and orating, she is a freelance artist and has a passion towards volleyball. MUNning has had a profound impact on the way she perceives the world and is keen on bringing the same perspective to everybody.

Vice-Chairperson

Rishaan R Ranka

Rishaan R Ranka is fascinated by geopolitics and space, things that constantly prompt him to think about the bigger picture and how the world, both above and below, operates. He started Munning in high school and has won multiple High Commendations, Honourable Mentions, and Verbal mentions. He believes that MUNs have played an essential role in shaping how he expresses himself, enabling him to enjoy the process of research, approach issues from multiple perspectives, and refine his oratory skills further. Beyond the debates and discussions, he opines that MUNs have also given him the chance to connect with people, exchange ideas, and build lasting friendships.

UNCOPUOS

Agenda:United Nations Committee on the Peaceful Uses of Outer Space

Chairperson

Atul Binosh Hegde

Atul Binosh Hegde, a 2nd-year B.Tech. student in Metallurgical and Materials Engineering at NIT Tiruchirappalli, is a proud alumnus of Yuvabharathi. He has previously served as the Secretary-General of YBMUN III and the Chairperson of WHO at YBMUN IV. He is delighted to return yet again this year as the Chair of UNCOPUOS. Having attended and chaired several local and national conferences over the past few years, winning various awards, he firmly believes that Model United Nations (MUN) is what has shaped him into the person he is today. Aside from MUNs and debating, he is an avid quizzer, writer, and word enthusiast, and enjoys travelling and exploring, often found cycling the streets of rural Coimbatore at midnight.

Vice-Chairperson

Haritha J

Haritha aspires to become a neurosurgeon. Having spent the last 5 years living and breathing diplomacy, Haritha is no stranger to challenging agendas or late-night resolutions. With experience in over 8 Security Council committees, she brings clarity and composure to the table. Beyond the podium, Haritha is a decorated athlete, having won medals in interschool handball and track events. She has won multiple accolades, including three best delegate awards and three high commendations in a span of 9 MUNs. She believes that global peace is achievable - provided the arguments are loud enough.

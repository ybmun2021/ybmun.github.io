Secretary-General

Atul Binosh Hegde

Atul, a grade 12 student at Yuvabharathi, has been passionate about performing and writing plays since his early years. With his incredible talent for weaving stories about warriors and dragons battling fiercely, he believes MUNs are an excellent platform for him to showcase his skills. Atul considers each committee an opportunity for role-play, where delegates strive to deliver their allotted roles perfectly. He is particularly fond of crisis committees, where he enjoys adapting to new situations and coming up with ideas on the fly in the form of directives. Atul's extensive experience with role-playing games and reading about wars has bolstered his skills. He has a profound interest in mathematics and spends his leisure time reading, running, and experimenting with new dishes in the kitchen.

Secretary-General

Nishi Kumari M

Nishi is a grade 12 student at Yuvabharathi who attended her first Model United Nations (MUN) conference in the 7th grade. She feels that her decision to join MUN has by far been the best decision she has ever made since she is passionate about debating. Besides debating, she enjoys dancing and reading books that transport her to different worlds. She can often be found playing volleyball, enjoying the spirit of teamwork, or on the skating rink, taking calculated risks and being determined to complete what she started. Additionally, she has a strong interest in numbers and aspires to become a Chartered Accountant.

Director-General

Kavya S A

Kavya, a grade 10 student at Yuvabharathi, has been an active participant in MUN ever since her middle school years, and she enjoys them a lot. Kavya believes that MUN has helped her expand her knowledge in every way possible, all while managing to weave in fun and intrigue. She is also an athlete and participates in various athletic meets as a javelin thrower. Kavya is also a part of the school's football team. In her leisure time, she enjoys philately and reading books. Kavya aspires to be someone who can bring about positive change in society.

Director-General

Hrithik Narayan V

Hrithik, a grade 9 student at Yuvabharathi, is an avid reader and speaker. He was captivated by the allure of MUN (Model United Nations) early on. Hrithik's keen interest in MUN led him to win the 'Best First Timer' award, significantly boosting his confidence. The following year, he won the "Best Delegate" award.

Apart from MUN, Hrithik is also interested in STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics) and aspires to become an entrepreneur. His musical journey, expressed through keyboard melodies, intertwines with pursuits like chess and football, forming a harmonious blend of passion and leisure.

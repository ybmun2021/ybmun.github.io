Secretary-General

Kavya S A

Kavya, a grade 12 student at Yuvabharathi, aspires to become a 'Change Maker' and pursue a career in the medical field. She believes that the ability to serve the community with one's knowledge is the greatest blessing one could have. Participating in MUNs developed a strong interest in diplomacy and global affairs in her. Whether it's through healing individuals or bridging nations, she wants her work to reflect empathy and a sense of purpose. The thrill of the debate, the power of representing a nation and the very beauty of the decorum drew her to MUNning. She received multiple Best Delegate awards and has taken on diverse roles at YBMUN, including delegate, a member of the Executive Board, and a member of the Secretariat. Beyond academics, she served as the Captain of Cedar House and represented the school in football, track and field, and javelin. She enjoys a myriad of interests, including reading, quilling, philately, photography and editing.

 

Director-General

Vaibhav R

Vaibhav, a grade 11 student at Yuvabharathi, aspires to major in astrophysics. As a person who loves to explore new things and try out new experiences, he has a wide array of interests. One of the most prominent of them is public speaking, and his other interest is music, specifically Carnatic and Western, which he believes is a significant part of his life. Kicking off MUN journey with a best-first timer in Grade 9, he bagged many Best Delegate awards and Special Mentions. MUNs made him feel more confident in networking with people. He is the President of the MUN club and actively contributes to honing the skills of MUNning aspirants. His interests also include reading books, callisthenics, playing tennis, running and yoga. He has won Distinction in IBT Science and English and represented the school as an ambassador in various social immersion programmes.

Director-General

Mythili R K

Mythili, a grade 11 student at Yuvabharathi, aspires to master her skills in management. She participated in MUNs since grade 7, and the very first experience of diplomacy and negotiation created a craving for debating and developed an interest towards MUN, which eventually fetched her High Commendation and Special mentions in various MUNs. Under-Secretary-General of Delegate Affairs

Gaurav Agarwal

Gaurav Agarwal, a grade 10 student at Yuvabharathi, set his goal in life to become "the person who had changed the course of history forever and left millions inspired". He aspires to become that all-rounder, someone with strong grades, athletic achievements, and a diverse portfolio. He started his MUN Journey in grade 7 and finally discovered his forte. MUNs became a platform where he could combine his love for public speaking with critical thinking and global affairs. He is driven by a deep curiosity for knowledge and a desire to lead, speak up, express, and engage. His consistent efforts have earned him many awards, including four Best Delegate awards, a high commendation, and three Special Mentions. He has won accolades in Olympiads and quizzes and is currently serving as the Vice-Captain of Cedar House. He is an avid reader of non-fiction and a passionate film enthusiast, a perpetual learner who is always eager to learn from the world around him.

Under-Secretary-General of Delegate Training

Moksh V Bhandari

Moksh V Bhandari, a grade 11 student at Yuvabharathi, aspires to become both a Financial Analyst and a Philanthropist. An organised extroverted person who likes to talk to people and find perfection in everything, he started his MUN journey in grade 9 and has never stopped since. He likes the adrenaline rush he gets from fast-paced committees like DISEC and AIPPM and believes that MUN has a massive role in shaping him for who he is today, a spontaneous and outgoing person. Having won Best Delegate, High Commendation, and Special Mention awards across numerous conferences, he believes that "you win some, you lose some, but end up learning in every one of them". His other interests include reading, writing poetry, nature photography, playing table tennis, and watching thriller pieces of writing, Agatha Christie being one of his favourite authors.

 

Under-Secretary-General of Hospitality & Logistics

Sai Nithin V

Sai Nithin, a grade 10 student at Yuvabharathi, aspires to serve society and become an entrepreneur. He describes himself as an enthusiastic individual who has a passion for quizzing and debating, and is interested in experimenting with new ideas and engaging in intellectually stimulating discussions. He started MUNning in his 7th grade, and ever since winning the 'Best First Timer' award, he has bagged six Best Delegate awards, a High Commendation, and three Special Mentions. He believes that listening to music instils inspiration and relaxation in him. Additionally, he finds joy in playing badminton, which keeps him active and fosters a sense of discipline and teamwork.

Secretary-General

Dhairya Bagri

Dhairya Bagri, a grade 11 student at Yuvabharathi, aspires to become a successful corporate lawyer with a firm of his own. He began MUNning back in 2021 when he was in the 8th grade. Being an avid quizzer, he finds current affairs his forte. MUNs provide him a platform to find solutions to current problems and become aware of the human side. His interest lies in Crisis committees, which are highly dynamic and spontaneous. He has participated in many MUNS, winning the Best Delegate awards, High Commendations and Special Mentions. He has served as the vice chair and the head delegate of the school. He claims that efficiency, composure, logicality and attention to detail are his four foundation pillars, and he believes that MUNs have helped him sharpen them.

Dhairya is the Captain of Pine House at Yuvabharathi and has podium finishes in football, hockey, basketball and cricket. His hobbies include baking and following cricket and football matches throughout the year.

 

Director-General

Adithi N

Adithi, a grade 12 student at Yuvabharathi, aspires to fuse her passion for journalism with International Business and Economics. At MUNs, every time she represents a country, she feels the vigour and zeal to establish maximum excellence in not only the fort of diplomacy but also learning. She believes MUNs are a way to gain opinions on intricate international affairs. She has won the Best Delegate awards and values every experience as nothing short of a profound learning opportunity.

Adithi enjoys indulging in different genres of books and is interested in exploring different music domains. She has mastered the skill of weaving any emotion or thought into an article, an essay, or a poem. Running marathons and hiking as an interest helps her build and practise resilience. She has published articles, headed the school's interschool debating event and was a member of the school football team.

Director-General

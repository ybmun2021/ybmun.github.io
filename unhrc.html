Chairperson

Pranav Manoj

A third-year Data Science student at IISER Thiruvananthapuram, Pranav brings together analytical thinking, diplomacy, and creativity to every committee he serves. Often called a "jack of all trades," Pranav Manoj is a passionate MUNner who believes in the transformative power of words. "Words are the most powerful magic, and they can change the world in either direction" is a principle he holds close to his heart.
With a deep love for Indian history and international geopolitics, Pranav's journey in Model UN has been one of learning and growth, instilling in him the values of diplomacy, negotiation, and problem-solving. Known for his fun-loving nature and ability to connect with people from all walks of life, he aims to be a socially responsible citizen, fostering positive change and working towards a better society.

Vice-Chairperson

Adithya V

Aadees M has taken on key roles, such as Director General and Vice Chair, in previous editions of YBMUN. Having been a part of multiple MUNs and speaking events, Aadees has been fortunate to receive recognitions, including several Best Delegate awards and a High Commendation, as well as serve on the Executive Board in conferences -successes he credits to the support of mentors, peers, and his spirit. With a passion for public speaking, communication, and litigation, Aadees hopes to make a positive difference - if not in the world, then at least in the life of a single person.

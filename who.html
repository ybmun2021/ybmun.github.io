Chairperson

Dharshana Durai

Dharshana Durai is a seasoned MUNner with over 7 years of experience as a chairperson and delegate. As a professional emcee, debater, and keynote speaker, she passionately embraces diplomacy in every role. Her personality is comprised entirely of geopolitical jokes and an unwavering love for her guitar. Alongside her academic pursuits, she works full-time as a content writer, social media manager and also serves as a project manager and event planner for an NGO.

Vice-Chairperson

Maanya R Jain

Maanya R Jain began her MUN journey in Grade 9 and has since grown from a delegate to an EB member, also serving as Under-Secretary-General Public Relations Head at YBMUN IV. She values mentorship, inclusivity, and responsive crisis management, striving to create platforms where all delegates can engage and excel. As a firm believer in voicing her opinions, she views the Model United Nations as a perfect platform to share her views and learn from others. Beyond MUNs, Maanya aspires to pursue a Master's in Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning, and is passionate about reading, swimming, and travelling.
